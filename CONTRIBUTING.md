# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve the template.

## Ideas that are welcome

- New tabs or metrics that suit a monthly marketing review
- Prompts for more data sources (the Windsor.ai connector supports 300+)
- Fixes to formulas, formatting, or definitions
- Translations of the Guide and Definitions tabs

## How to contribute

1. Fork the repo and create a branch.
2. Make your change. If you edit the workbook, keep the formula-driven design and the Total Users convention intact.
3. Open a pull request with a short before-and-after note.

## Ground rules

- Keep the template brand-agnostic. No real account IDs, domains, or company names.
- One volume metric: Total Users. Do not reintroduce sessions as a headline metric.
- Keep every input cell blue and every formula cell grey or black.
